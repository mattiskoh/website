about me

I am passionate working in the gap between the digital and physical realms. With my background in mechanical engineering I enjoy exploring and working in the boundaries between architecture, engineering, construction (AEC), robotics, and design. My work spans from mechanical and mechatronic design; to writing code for computer simulations, digital fabrication; developing parametric design tools as well as programming industrial robots.

Currently I am working at MESH focusing on applying robotics to the world of rebar (reinforcement bar) in the construction sector.
