Foldable Sled

Sledding is fun, however before you can slide down from the top of a hill you have to go through a long and tedious process. This can be quite a pain, and discourages people to go sledding. For the course 'Interdisciplinary Product Development' at the ETH ZÃ¼rich and ZHdK we aimed to redesign the classic rodel sled by improving the user interaction around sledding. One of the main goals was to make the threshold of going sledding as low as possible. Folding the sled is done with a single fluid motion using only one hand. The folding mechanism is entirely integrated into the sled allowing it to fold completely flat due to its compliant hinges.
The final deliverable was a full-scale functioning prototype made out birch plywood, aluminium and webbing using CNC milling and additive manufacturing.

Made together with three industrial design students from Zurich University of Arts (ZHdK)
